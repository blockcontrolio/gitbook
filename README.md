# BlockControl Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **BlockControl** — a MiCAR-compliant, white-label stablecoin infrastructure platform built for European banks and Electronic Money Institutions (EMIs).

---

## What is BlockControl?

BlockControl enables licensed financial institutions to **issue, manage, and transfer their own branded Electronic Money Tokens (EMTs)** on public and permissioned blockchains — without building blockchain infrastructure from scratch.

Rather than competing with existing crypto-native issuers, BlockControl gives banks and EMIs the tools to operate as stablecoin issuers themselves, under their own brand and regulatory license.

## Key Capabilities

- **EMT Creation & Issuance** — Deploy EUR-pegged tokens on Arbitrum with full lifecycle management
- **Multi-Counterparty Architecture** — Manage banks, EMIs, distributors, and clients under one platform
- **Granular RBAC** — On-chain and off-chain role separation with least-privilege enforcement
- **Transfer Engine** — Internal, external, and inter-counterparty (inter-bank) transfer flows
- **MPC / WaaS Integration** — Non-custodial wallet infrastructure via Dfns
- **Invoice & Payment System** — Create, send, and process payments with deeplink support
- **Webhook & Async API** — Event-driven integration for production banking systems
- **MiCAR Compliance Ready** — Built to support Article 51 EMT regulatory requirements
